Something I find interesting is Fantasy Football. It has gotten more popular and traction over the years, as gambling has gotten more popular, but I must stress that gambling is NOT required to play the game! In fact, I have personally NEVER played in a league for money. Fantasy football can be played on many platforms, such as ESPN's Fantasy Football platform. The fundamental concept of the game is quite simple! Each week in the NFL, with the exception of a bye, each team will play 1 game. Therefore, you too will play 1 game. Through this, you can pick a team at the beginning of the season, and each week your players will play 1 game and score points for you based on their real life performance! At the end of the season, a playoffs are played and a winner is crowned, the glory of the championship is why we play!
